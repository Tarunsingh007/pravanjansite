Table Tennis

Table tennis, sometimes known as "ping pong" is a miniaturized version of outdoor or lawn tennis. A dynamic indoor racquets sport played world-wide; table tennis had an inauspicious beginning. The sport had its origins in England in the late 1800s as a parlour game known as "Whiff Waff," an activity played on dining room tables for recreation. Table tennis is played today in virtually every country of the world, with sanctioned competitions in age group categories ranging from under 10 years of age to 80 years and over. Table tennis is an official Paralympics wheelchair sport. There are men', women', doubles, and mixed doubles categories of play. BIET JHANSI has a separate assigned TT Hall with a huge space to accommodate 3 tables. Every hostel has a common hall, in which a TT table is provided which helps the students to brush up their skills at any time of the day. The college also provides TT racquets and balls which enables uninterrupted practice.

Carrom

Carrom is a board game played by 2 or 4 players against each other. The objective of carrom is to put all your carrom pieces before your opponent, using skill, determination and a little bit of luck. It is a scientific game and is based on physics and geometry. It requires thorough knowledge of angles, deftness of touch, keen sight and superlative control of nerves besides mental and physical fitness. It also requires skill and deep concentration. The All India Carrom Federation controls this game in India. The Federation has already organised 144 national championships and hundreds of other tournaments. Carrom runs in the STUDENT ACTIVITY CENTRE which has got 4 carrom boards with sufficient facilities for both men’s and women’s.

Badminton

Badminton, court game played with lightweight rackets and a shuttlecock. The new sport was definitively launched in 1873 at the Badminton House, Gloucestershire, owned by the Duke of Beaufort. Since 1992, badminton has been an Olympic sport with five events: men's and women's singles, men's and women's doubles, and mixed doubles, in which each pair is a man and a woman. BIET JHANSI is lashed with 4 wooden badminton courts, and 3 cemented courts, also we have dedicated badminton courts in every hostel.
